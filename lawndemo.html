About Lawnscape

For over 15 years, we've been transforming outdoor spaces across Southern California with our premium lawn care services.

Our Story

Lawnscape began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional lawn care services that transform ordinary yards into extraordinary outdoor spaces. Founded in 2008 by horticulture expert David Miller, we've grown from a small local operation to one of Southern California's most trusted lawn care companies.

Today, our team of certified professionals serves homeowners and businesses throughout Pasadena, Riverside, Los Angeles, and surrounding communities, combining cutting-edge techniques with environmentally responsible practices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our lawn care services.

In California's climate, we recommend mowing your lawn every 7-10 days during the growing season (spring and summer) and every 2-3 weeks during cooler months (fall and winter). This frequency helps maintain optimal grass height and promotes healthy growth.

Yes, we offer organic and eco-friendly lawn care solutions that are safe for children, pets, and the environment. Our organic program uses natural fertilizers, biological pest control, and sustainable practices to maintain a healthy lawn without synthetic chemicals.

We provide lawn care services throughout Southern California, including Pasadena, Riverside, Los Angeles, and surrounding communities. If you're unsure whether we service your area, please enter your ZIP code in our service checker at the top of the page or contact us directly.

Scheduling a service is easy! You can fill out any of the quote forms on our website, call us directly at (555) 123-4567, or use our online booking system. Once we receive your request, we'll contact you within 24 hours to discuss your needs and schedule a convenient time for your service.

If weather conditions prevent us from performing your scheduled service, we'll contact you to reschedule for the next available day. We typically resume services as soon as conditions allow, usually within 24-48 hours after rain stops, depending on grass dryness.

No, you don't need to be home during our service visits. As long as we have access to your lawn and any necessary gates are unlocked, our team can perform the service while you're away. We'll send you before and after photos and a service summary after completion.
