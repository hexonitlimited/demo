Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our lawn care services.

For most lawns in California, we recommend weekly mowing during the growing season (spring and summer) and bi-weekly during fall. Winter mowing needs are minimal but may be required depending on weather conditions.

Yes, we use pet and child-friendly products that are EPA-approved. We always provide specific instructions for each treatment, but generally, we recommend keeping pets and children off the lawn until the product has dried (typically 2-4 hours).

We primarily serve Pasadena, Riverside, Los Angeles, and surrounding communities in Southern California. Contact us with your address, and we'll let you know if we service your area.

We offer both one-time services and recurring maintenance plans. Our recurring plans offer discounted pricing and priority scheduling.

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and checks. For recurring services, we also offer automatic payment options for your convenience.

We understand that schedules change. You can reschedule or cancel your appointment with at least 24 hours notice without any penalty. For same-day cancellations, a small fee may apply.
