About Lawnscape

Founded in 2015 in Pasadena, Lawnscape has grown from a small local operation to one of California's most trusted lawn care providers. Our mission is simple: deliver exceptional lawn care services that transform outdoor spaces while using eco-friendly practices.

With a team of licensed professionals and a commitment to customer satisfaction, we've built a reputation for reliability, quality, and affordability. We understand that your lawn is an extension of your home, and we treat it with the care and attention it deserves.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive lawn care services to keep your outdoor space looking its best year-round.

Lawn Mowing

Professional lawn mowing services with precise cutting heights and clean lines to enhance your property's curb appeal.

Edging & Trimming

Detail-oriented edging along walkways, driveways, and flower beds, plus precise trimming around obstacles.

Weed Control

Targeted weed management using eco-friendly products to eliminate unwanted plants without harming your lawn.

Fertilization

Customized fertilization programs to nourish your lawn, promote healthy growth, and enhance color and density.

Seasonal Cleanup

Comprehensive cleanup services in spring and fall to remove debris, thatch, and prepare your lawn for the season.

Aeration

Core aeration services to reduce soil compaction, improve water absorption, and promote root growth.

Commercial Property Maintenance

Comprehensive lawn care solutions for businesses, HOAs, and commercial properties with flexible scheduling.

Landscaping Enhancements

Custom landscaping services including mulching, flower bed installation, and hardscape improvements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our lawn care services.

For most lawns in California, we recommend mowing every 7-10 days during the growing season (spring through fall) and every 2-3 weeks during the winter. However, the ideal frequency depends on your grass type, growth rate, and personal preference. Our team can help you determine the best schedule for your lawn.

Yes, we prioritize the safety of your family and pets. We use eco-friendly, EPA-approved products that are safe when applied correctly. For treatments that require temporary precautions (such as fertilization or pest control), we'll provide clear instructions on how long to keep pets and children off the treated areas.

No, you don't need to be home for most of our services. We can access your yard as long as gates are unlocked and pets are kept indoors. For your first service, we recommend being home to discuss any specific requirements. After that, we can service your lawn while you're away, and we'll send you a summary of the work completed.

If weather conditions prevent us from safely servicing your lawn, we'll reschedule for the next available day. We'll notify you as early as possible about any changes to your schedule. For light rain, we may still be able to perform certain services, but heavy rain, lightning, or extremely wet conditions will require rescheduling.

We offer several convenient payment options. You can pay by credit card, debit card, or bank transfer through our secure online portal. For ongoing services, we also offer automatic billing options. Invoices are sent after each service is completed, with payment due within 7 days.

Yes, we stand behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our service, contact us within 48 hours, and we'll make it right. This includes returning to re-mow missed areas, addressing weed control issues, or making any necessary adjustments to ensure your lawn meets your expectations.
