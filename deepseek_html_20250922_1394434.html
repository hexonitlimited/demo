Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our lawn care services in Pasadena, Riverside, and Los Angeles.

Our lawn care services start at $35 for small properties and vary based on lawn size, condition, and specific services needed. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. For a detailed quote tailored to your property, please check our pricing page or contact us for a free estimate.

We provide lawn care services throughout Pasadena, Riverside, Los Angeles, and surrounding communities in California. If you're unsure whether we service your specific area, please contact us with your zip code, and we'll be happy to confirm availability in your neighborhood.

Yes, we prioritize eco-friendly lawn care solutions. We use sustainable practices and environmentally safe products whenever possible. Our team is trained in integrated pest management techniques that minimize chemical use while maintaining effective results. We're happy to discuss our eco-friendly options for your specific lawn care needs.

For most lawns in California, we recommend weekly mowing during the peak growing season (spring and early summer) and bi-weekly during slower growth periods. However, the ideal frequency depends on your grass type, weather conditions, and personal preferences. Our team can recommend a customized schedule based on your lawn's specific needs.

Yes, we offer flexible subscription plans for ongoing lawn care services. Our subscription options include weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly service frequencies. Subscribers receive priority scheduling, discounted rates compared to one-time services, and automatic seasonal adjustments to their lawn care program. You can easily modify or cancel your subscription with 48 hours' notice.

We understand that plans change. For one-time services, please provide at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule without penalty. For subscription plans, we require 48 hours' notice before your next scheduled service. Cancellations made with less notice may be subject to a cancellation fee. We strive to be flexible and work with our customers to accommodate schedule changes whenever possible.
