Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our lawn care services.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve Pasadena, Riverside, Los Angeles, and surrounding communities in Southern California. Contact us to see if your area is within our service range.

How often should I have my lawn serviced?

This depends on your lawn's specific needs, the season, and your preferences. Most customers opt for weekly or bi-weekly service during peak growing seasons, and monthly during slower periods. We can assess your lawn and recommend the ideal schedule.

Are your products safe for pets and children?

Yes, we prioritize the safety of your family and pets. We use EPA-approved products and follow all label instructions for application. We also offer organic and pet-friendly options upon request.

What if it rains on my scheduled service day?

We monitor weather conditions closely. If rain is expected on your service day, we'll contact you to reschedule. Light rain typically doesn't affect most services, but heavy rain may require postponement for optimal results.

Do I need to be home during service?

No, you don't need to be home. We can access your yard as long as gates are unlocked. We'll send you a notification when we arrive and complete the service, along with before/after photos if requested.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, checks, and cash. For recurring services, we offer automatic billing for your convenience.

Can I skip or reschedule a service?

Yes, you can reschedule or skip a service with at least 24 hours notice. Simply contact our customer service team, and we'll accommodate your request.

What's included in your basic lawn care service?

Our basic service includes mowing, edging, blowing off hard surfaces, and a general cleanup. Additional services like trimming, fertilization, weed control, and aeration are available as add-ons or in our premium packages.
