About Lawnscape

Founded in 2015 in Pasadena, Lawnscape has grown from a small local operation to California's premier lawn care service. Our team of certified professionals is committed to delivering exceptional results while using eco-friendly practices that protect our environment.

We believe that every lawn deserves to look its best. That's why we offer comprehensive services tailored to the unique needs of California's diverse landscapes. From residential lawns to commercial properties, we bring expertise, reliability, and a passion for perfection to every job.

Frequently Asked Questions

For most lawns in California, we recommend mowing every 7-10 days during the growing season (spring through fall). During winter months, you can reduce frequency to every 2-3 weeks. The exact timing depends on your grass type, weather conditions, and how quickly your lawn grows.

Our standard lawn care service includes mowing, edging along sidewalks and driveways, trimming around obstacles, and blowing grass clippings off hard surfaces. We also offer additional services like fertilization, weed control, aeration, and seasonal cleanup as part of our comprehensive lawn care packages.

No, you don't need to be home during our service visits. As long as we have access to your lawn (gates unlocked, pets secured, etc.), our team can complete the work while you're away. We'll send you before and after photos so you can see the results of our work.

Our pricing is based on several factors including lawn size, terrain complexity, service frequency, and specific services requested. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. You can use our online price calculator for an estimate, or contact us for a customized quote based on your specific needs.

We primarily service the greater Los Angeles area, including Pasadena, Riverside, and surrounding communities. If you're unsure whether we service your area, please enter your ZIP code in our service area checker or contact us directly. We're continuously expanding our service area to meet customer demand.

Yes, we prioritize the safety of your family and pets. We use eco-friendly products that are safe when applied correctly. For any treatments that require temporary restrictions (like fertilization or weed control), we'll provide clear instructions on when it's safe for pets and children to return to the treated areas.

If weather conditions prevent us from safely servicing your lawn, we'll reschedule your service for the next available day. We'll notify you of any schedule changes via text or email. Our team monitors weather forecasts closely to minimize disruptions to your service schedule.

You can cancel or reschedule your service by calling our office, using our online customer portal, or replying to your service reminder email/text. We request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling to avoid any fees. Our team is happy to work with you to find a new service time that fits your schedule.
