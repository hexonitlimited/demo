About Lawnscape

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Lawnscape began with a simple mission: to provide California homeowners and businesses with premium lawn care services that deliver exceptional results. What started as a small, local operation has grown into a trusted name in lawn maintenance throughout the state.

Our team of certified professionals combines expertise with a passion for creating beautiful, healthy outdoor spaces. We use eco-friendly practices and state-of-the-art equipment to ensure your lawn looks its best while minimizing environmental impact.
