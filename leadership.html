90 min • Interactive • Action-driven

Icebreaker (Chat): “Who is a leader you admire and why? Type in chat now!”

⚖️ Leadership VS Authority

Position = power given. Influence = power earned.

Scenario: A volunteer notices food shortage at a VBD event, no coordinator around. They arrange extra supplies and communicate with vendors. Was this leadership? Why?
💡 Script: “Click raise hand or type YES/NO in chat. This is the heart of titleless leadership.”
💬 Vote: Yes, leadership!

Mover
Drives action
Follower
Supports execution
Opposer
Challenges critically
Bystander
Disengaged

💡 Discuss strengths & risks: Opposer brings needed checks, Bystander loses opportunity.

🗣️ Communication & Conflict

Active Listening • Respectful Disagreement

Scenario: Two VBD volunteers disagree on an event layout – one wants stalls inside, the other outside. Team leader steps in. What should leader do?
📢 Chat: “Type conflict resolution idea (listen, find middle ground, vote)”. Then share: “Seek interests not positions.”
💡 Resolution framework: 1. Listen to both 2. Acknowledge 3. Propose hybrid solution.

⏳ 7-Day Leadership Challenge

Identify 1 problem you will solve this week (at home/college/volunteer project)
1 initiative you will take without being asked
1 leadership behavior (e.g., listening more, offering help)
📝 Action: Write in your notebook / Google Keep. Share one intention in chat.
🔥 Example: “I’ll lead the class cleanup, start a study group, or thank a teammate publicly.”
