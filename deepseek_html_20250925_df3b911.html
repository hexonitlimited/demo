About Lawnscape

Our Story

Founded in 2015 in Pasadena, Lawnscape has grown from a small local operation to one of California's most trusted lawn care providers. Our mission is simple: deliver exceptional lawn care services that transform outdoor spaces while respecting our environment.

With a team of licensed professionals, state-of-the-art equipment, and eco-friendly practices, we've helped thousands of California homeowners and businesses achieve the lush, healthy lawns they deserve.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive lawn care services to keep your outdoor spaces looking their best year-round.

Lawn Mowing

Professional mowing services with precise cutting heights and clean lines for a perfectly manicured lawn.

Edging & Trimming

Detailed edging along walkways, driveways, and flower beds plus trimming of hard-to-reach areas.

Weed Control

Targeted weed treatments to eliminate unwanted plants while protecting your lawn's health and appearance.

Fertilization

Customized fertilization programs using premium products to promote lush, green growth and root development.

Seasonal Cleanup

Comprehensive cleanup services in spring and fall to prepare your lawn for the changing seasons.

Aeration

Core aeration services to reduce soil compaction and improve water, nutrient, and oxygen penetration.

Commercial Property Maintenance

Comprehensive lawn care and landscaping solutions for businesses, HOAs, and commercial properties.

Landscaping Enhancements

Custom landscaping solutions including garden design, planting, mulching, and hardscape features.
